Toad's Tonics

Toad's Tonics is a crafting-type video game. Players will play as Toad and complete potion orders for Toad's neighboring woodland friends. Each potion has a recipe, and if the player has the right ingredients, they can play a short mini-game to create the potion. The higher the mini-game score, the higher the grade of the potion (S grade, A+ grade, A grade, A- grade, B+ grade, etc.). Customers will be willing to spend more flies for higher grade potions. Toad can then spend his flies at the store to unlock new potion recipes and buy more ingredients.

  • Created a visual composition for a potential crafting game including a main menu, a settings and accessibility menu, pause screen, and store menu
  • Completed composition and wireframe in Adobe XD
  • Designed and drew all icons such as Toad, the recipe book, the potions, and the ingredients

Rainbow Runes

Rainbow Runes is a puzzle 2D platformer game. A dog has accidentally stepped through a portal to another world, and he needs to reactive the portal to return home. To reactivate the portal, the player must activate runes in a specific order while avoiding enemies and traps.

  • 2D puzzle platform game created in Unity utilizing tilemaps and tile palettes
  • Players must complete the puzzle of activating runes in a specific order (rainbow order)
  • Includes enemies, a pickup item that activates a double jump, traps, shakeable tiles, and an NPC with a dialogue system
  • Created all sprites, backgrounds, and animations in FireAlpaca

Spring to Summer

Spring to Summer is a 2D shooter. Players play as a weather fairy that must defeat spring-type obstacles (such as bees, flowers, pollen, and rain clouds) in order to make way for summer.

  • 2D shooter game created in Unity
  • Includes stationary enemies, shooter enemies, and chaser enemies
  • Complete with music and sound effects
  • Created all sprites, backgrounds, and animations in FireAlpaca
